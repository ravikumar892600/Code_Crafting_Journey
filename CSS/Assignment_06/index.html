Gulam Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Make sugar syrup: Boil 1.5 cups sugar with 1.5 cups water until one-string consistency.
  2. Prepare dough: Mix dry ingredients, add ghee and milk to form a soft dough.
  3. Roll into balls, fry till golden, soak in sugar syrup.
  4. Rest for 1-2 hours, garnish, and enjoy Gulab Jamun!
